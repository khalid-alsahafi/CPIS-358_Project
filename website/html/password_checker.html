General recommendations when choosing a passowrd:

  • The longer your password the better.
  • Use upper-case and lower-case letters.
  • Use numbers and symbols (0 to 9 and ~`! @#$%^&*()_-+={[}]|\:;"'<,>.?/)
  • Don't use characters/numbers in asending or descending orders (e.g., "abcd" or "4321").
  • Don't use your name age or birthday.
